Pillars of Wellness

Evidence-based wellness metrics for improving health outcomes

Wellness Aspect Key Metric Recommended Value Health Impact Supporting Research
Sleep Duration Hours per night 7-9 hours Reduces chronic disease risk by 30% National Sleep Foundation, 2022
Physical Activity Minutes per week 150-300 minutes Lowers cardiovascular mortality by 35% WHO Guidelines, 2023
Water Consumption Liters per day 2.7-3.7 liters Improves cognitive function by 25% Journal of Nutrition, 2022
Meditation Practice Minutes per day 10-20 minutes Reduces stress hormones by 28% JAMA Internal Medicine, 2021
Fruit & Vegetable Intake Servings per day 5-9 servings Decreases all-cause mortality by 23% American Heart Association, 2023
Social Connection Meaningful interactions/week 3-5 quality interactions Increases longevity by 50% Harvard Study of Adult Development
Screen Time Reduction Hours before bed 1-2 hours Improves sleep quality by 45% Sleep Research Society, 2022
